Music Festival Poster

The Music Festival project is a one month project with a poster and an augmented reality experience made with Adobe Aero.

Elevate is jazz fusion music festival that is designed to be a calm, trippy escape from reality, with low elevator music reverberating around you while you stare at the sky, stars, and sit by the water on the beach.
