Plara Plant App Design

Plara is a 4 month project that had 3 months of User research followed by a month of UI design and refinement.

It's a fun plant app for every kind of plant parent. Simple, easy to use, and community based, Plara aims to create a friendly inclusive environment for you to be your best plant parent self!
